Frequently Asked Questions

What is Airbnb In-room dining?
  • Airbnb In-room dining is a B2B platform (web & app) that connects Airbnb hosts, co-hosts, and agents managing guest units with a live platform that enables them to earn commissions from in-room dining purchases made by guests.

  • The service is seamless whereby edibles are delivered to the guests by the in-room dining service, and after the purchase is made, commissions are automatically calculated on the back end.
How does Airbnb In-room dining work for merchants or partners?
  • Signing your Airbnb unit to the app is easy, you just have to fill in the form.
How long does it take to become a partner?
  • Your Airbnb onboarding can usually go live within 5 working days as long as you send us all the details.

  • You can do this online, quickly and safely.
How will I expose my guests to In-room dining menu?
  • This can be done through creating awareness upon guest check-in, use of printed posters and leaflets, advert on the WI-FI poster, availability of a physical menu in the room, QR code adverts etc

  • We will provide you with marketing material, discount promo codes, QR code link, and menu displays which will be customizable according to your chosen theme.

  • We will also include a 24/7 customer care support line in case a guest would like to order via phone call/WhatsApp.
How will I be able to view live sales?
  • Upon becoming a partner, we will share login credentials using the primary email and promo code you registered with.

  • You will be able to see all the live sales from your backend.
